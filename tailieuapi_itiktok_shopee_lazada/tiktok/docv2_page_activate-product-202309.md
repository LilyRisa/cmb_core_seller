Activate Product

‚ÄčShop

‚Äč

Required scope:

‚Äčseller.product.basic

‚Äč

Activate hidden products that are in the Seller\_deactivated or Platform\_deactivated status and display them in the TikTok Shop catalog. ‚Äč

After submitting the activation request, the products will be sent to TikTok Shop for auditing and their status will change to Pending. If a product passes the audit, its status will change back to Activate. You can use the [Product status change webhook](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/650956aff1fd3102b90b6261) to monitor the audit status.‚Äč
