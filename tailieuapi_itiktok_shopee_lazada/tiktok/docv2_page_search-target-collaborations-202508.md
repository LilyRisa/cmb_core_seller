creator\_accept\_statusвЂ‹

 | 

stringвЂ‹

 | 

Filtering by acceptance status of creators who are invited to target collaborations.вЂ‹

You can query based on:вЂ‹

\- "ACCEPT", it means creators have already added the products to the showcaseвЂ‹

\- "ALL""", it means regardless of whether the creator adds the product to the Showcase.вЂ‹

By default, the target collaborations will be searched by "ALL" status.вЂ‹

| 

collaboration\_statusвЂ‹

RequiredвЂ‹

 | 

stringвЂ‹

 | 

Filtering by target collaborations' status.вЂ‹

You can query based on:вЂ‹

\- ONGOING: If you use ONGOING to search, the remaining validity period of the returned target collaborations will be greater than 7 days.вЂ‹

\- EXPIRING: If you use EXPIRING to search, the remaining validity period of the returned target collaborations will be less than 7 days.вЂ‹

\- VALID: If you use VALID to search, all returned target collaborations will be valid.вЂ‹

\- CANCELING: If you use CANCELING to search, all returned target collaborations will be in the process of canceling.вЂ‹

\- COMPLETED: If you use COMPLETED to search, all returned target collaborations will be in the state of completed, including expired and canceled.вЂ‹
