settle\_statusвЂ‹

 | 

intвЂ‹

 | 

Settlement Status of the order.вЂ‹

Possible values:вЂ‹

1\. "CUSTOMER UNPAID", customer hasn't paid for the orders.вЂ‹

2\. "PENDING", customers can still return or get a refund for this order. The earnings shown are estimates and waiting for settlement.вЂ‹

3\. "SETTLED", your earnings have already settled to your accountвЂ‹

4\. "INELIGIBLE", The order has been returned/refunded/canceled by the buyer, and no commission will be settled.вЂ‹

5\. "FROZEN", Possible fraud has been detected regarding the order. The commission will be unfrozen after the fraud is resolved.вЂ‹

 |

Filter orders to show only those that are created on or after the specified date and time. Unix timestamp.вЂ‹

create\_time\_ge and create\_time\_lt must be provided together. If both are not specified, data from the last three months will be queried by default.вЂ‹
