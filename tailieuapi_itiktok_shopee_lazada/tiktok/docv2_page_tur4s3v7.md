# What is settlement report?

A settlement report is a financial document that provides a detailed breakdown of your account activity for a specified settlement period. This report enables you to reconcile your commission earned.

# How can I download the settlement report?

To download the settlement report, please follow these steps:  
Partner Center-Finance-Payment-Earnings-Export orders.
