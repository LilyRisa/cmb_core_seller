**What Are the Benefits?**

-   **For New partners:** Accelerate your business launch with milestone-driven guidance across the entire lifecycle: from customer acquisition (publishing service listings) and contract signing to promotion (creating campaigns) and settlement.
    
-   **For** **Existing partners:** Optimize daily operations through data-driven efficiency: Monitor key metrics at a glance, prioritize urgent to-dos (e.g., expiring contracts, sample requests), and unlock growth opportunities via task completion.

#### Display Rules

-   **Sub Account Access Control**

ToDo/data modules only display if sub-accounts have corresponding menu permissions.  
e.g. if the permissions of the sub-account include the Sample request page, the corresponding pending tasks will be displayed; if the sub-account permissions include the Shop GMV menu permission of Data Compass, the corresponding data module will be displayed.
