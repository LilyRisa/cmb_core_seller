order\_idsвЂ‹

RequiredвЂ‹

 | 

\[\]stringвЂ‹

 | 

The IDs of all the orders sent in a single first-mile bundle.вЂ‹

The orders must follow the restrictions:вЂ‹

\- Each of the orders must exist and be RTS and shipping label printed.вЂ‹

\- The orders are sent by the same seller.вЂ‹

\- The orders belong to a single group of TikTok Shop service districts. The groups are:вЂ‹

\- Group 1: PH, SG, MY, VN, TH, and JP.вЂ‹

\- Group 2: DE, FR, IT, ES.вЂ‹

вЂ‹

You can not create first mile bundles for US/UK orders using this API.вЂ‹

 |
| 

handover\_methodвЂ‹

RequiredвЂ‹

 | 

stringвЂ‹

 | 

The way you send the first-mile bundle. Possible enumerations are:вЂ‹

\- PICKUP: You use the logistic service provided by TikTok Shop to send the bundle.вЂ‹

\- DROP\_OFF: You contact the logistic provider and send the bundle. The logistic provider must be registered at TikTok Shop.вЂ‹
