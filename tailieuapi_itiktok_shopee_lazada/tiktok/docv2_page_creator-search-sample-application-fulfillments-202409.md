fulfillment\_statusesвЂ‹

RequiredвЂ‹

 | 

\[\]stringвЂ‹

 | 

A list of fulfillment statuses. The response is filtered to include sample fulfillments with the fulfillment\_status field set to one of the specified values. The possible values are:вЂ‹

\- PENDING: The creator has not yet fulfilled the content creation obligation.вЂ‹

\- ONGOING: Fulfillment is in progress; content has been created and is being evaluated against criteria.вЂ‹

\- SUCCEED: Fulfillment has been successfully completed; the content meets the required standards.вЂ‹

\- FAILED: Fulfillment failed; the content did not meet the required standards.вЂ‹

\- OVERDUE: Fulfillment is overdue; the creator did not meet the deadline.вЂ‹

\- SUSPEND: Fulfillment has been suspended.вЂ‹

\- CANCELLED: Fulfillment has been cancelled, either by the creator or due to operational reasons.вЂ‹

\- EXEMPTED: The creator is exempt from the fulfillment obligation.вЂ‹
