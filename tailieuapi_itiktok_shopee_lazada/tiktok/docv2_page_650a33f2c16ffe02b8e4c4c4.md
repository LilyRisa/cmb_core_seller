## What is Finance？

Finance feature enables MCN& TAP to check and export orders and bills after sharing commission with Creator

## 【Payment account】Active account and links to bank account

## 【Earnings】Check Commission Order

You can check commission order under **\[earnings\]** tab  
Note:  
**\[earnings\]** tab will show all the commission orders from **the creators who have a commission settlement relationship with MCN**

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/796c9afe1a36453ba2c3b9c7251bf3a0~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

**Step 2 Check Commission Order**  
You can check the overview of **Commission Order** at the top，including

-   paid（successful paid orders),you can go to\[paid\] tab to check details
-   Not paid(including pending payment and payment failed orders ),you can go to \[pending payment\] and\[payment failed\] tab to check details

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/7ce974b2599643da9a0a93c2e3d9ae79~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

**Step 3 Check and export order details**

-   You can view the order details under \[Payment pending\], \[Payment made\], and \[Payment failed\].
-   You can filter by username, product id, order created time,order delivered time

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/3b02c63de2ef4ee885e70138b665a835~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

-   Click \[Export\] to support full export or batch export（Either choose filters or all order under current tab. **250000 orders maximum at once）**

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/ca57c7730d8e466cbf47f841215f7793~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)
