# Partner service categories

> Source: https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/64f1989064ed2e0295f3c1b5
> Section: Partner Guide
> Scraped: 2026-05-21T00:12:55.074Z

---

Service categories are predefined groupings that help organize and categorize the various services offered by partners on TikTok Shop Partner Center. These categories make it easier for TikTok Shop sellers to find relevant services that align with their needs and goals. Partners can select the appropriate service category when creating a service to ensure it's listed in the relevant section of the App & Service Store, making it more discoverable to potential clients.  
📌 The service categories available to a partner are determined by several factors, including the partner's registration region and their chosen target market. This means that partners will only see service categories that are relevant to their region and target market when creating services on TikTok Shop Partner Center. This helps ensure that partners can offer services that are appropriate for their specific business context and geographic focus.
