# What is TikTok Shop Partner (TSP) ？

TikTok Shop Partners (TSPs) offer a range of services to sellers, including operational mentoring, content creation, and shop management, enabling sellers to scale their businesses on TikTok Shop effectively.  
Click to learn more about [TSP service categories](64f1989064ed2e0295f3c1b5#TSP)

# What are the benefits of becoming a TSP on TikTok Shop？

**New Leads**

-   Have the opportunity to be recommended to suitable sellers and brands

**Service Market Discovery**

-   Increase your visibility as sellers discover your services in the App & Service Store.

**Rewards and Incentives**

-   Take advantage of various incentive policies, including financial rewards and traffic.

**Training and Development**

-   Participate in regular training and partner development programs to accelerate your business growth

**Custom Services**

-   High-performing TikTok Shop Partners receive personalized one-to-one support from our partner operations team.
