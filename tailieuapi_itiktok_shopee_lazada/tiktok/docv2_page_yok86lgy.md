In this article, we’ll introduce our latest feature that allows you to connect your TikTok business account in Partner Center to boost your brand, build trust, and streamline creator recruitment.  
After successfully linking your account, you’ll be able to display official business and partner information—whether you’re a **TikTok Shop Multi-Channel Network (MCN)** or **TikTok Shop Affiliate Partner (TAP)**—on your TikTok business account.

## How to Link Your TikTok Business Account in Partner Center

1.  Go to [Partner Center > My Account](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/approval/profile) and click **Link TikTok Account**.

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/b9bc84abc2f042eab3cc9947498db04c~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

1.  Follow on-screen guidance. If you don’t have a TikTok account, click **Sign Up for TikTok** to create one. If you have an existing account, click **Get Started** to retrieve your QR code.

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/ed7597a618d742779e00bb8c7635c0f4~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

1.  Open TikTok app, go to your profile, tap the top right button, select **My QR code**, and scan the code in Partner Center and tap **Connect Now** on TikTok app to complete the process.

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/8594adcf8ee3444690823ad2930eb3f3~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

**❗️Note:** Ensure you link the correct TikTok account. If you wish to unlink or change your TikTok account, please contact your account manager.

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/3a54c3e07f4d492da9ac8615d9c96ef3~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

## How to Attach Your TikTok Handle to Your Invitation

After successfully linking your TikTok business account to Partner Center, you will be able to attach it to your invitations. Creators and sellers will be able to view your TikTok business account through various journeys:

### Manage Partnerships

To initiate a binding relationship with creators, go to [Manage Partnerships > Add New Creator](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/affiliate-creator/binding-creator?market=4&tab=all). On this page, select **Attach TikTok profile** to include your TikTok handle.

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/25ea5168b85f4c5faf7a51a62df7278a~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

### Find Creators

If you want to invite creators for product collaboration, go to [Find Creators > Invite](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/affiliate-cmp/creator?market=4). On this page, select **Attach TikTok profile** to include your TikTok handle.

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/0c474ce0aa984f429767fa1f151bdd72~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

### Partner Campaign

To initiate a partner campaign, go to [Partner Campaigns > Campaigns](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/affiliate-campaign/campaign) and click **Create Campaign**. Scroll down to the page to the **Social Account** section, select **Attach TikTok profile** to include your TikTok handle.

## FAQ

1.  **Can I link multiple TikTok business accounts to the same Partner Center account?**

No, you can only link one TikTok business account to each Partner Center account. If you need to manage multiple TikTok business accounts, you’ll need separate Partner Center accounts for each.

1.  **What happens if I accidentally link the wrong TikTok account?**

If you link the wrong TikTok account, you’ll need to contact your account manager to unlink it. Once unlinked, you can go through the linking process again with the correct account.

1.  **What should I do if I encounter an error while linking my TikTok account?**

If you encounter an error when linking your account, ensure your TikTok account meets the following criteria:

-   The TikTok account holder must be at least 18 years old.
-   The status of the TikTok account must be active and have no violation history.
-   The TikTok account cannot be a registered business account.
-   The TikTok account cannot be linked with another agency or the Creator Network.

If all criteria are met and the error persists, contact your account manager for further assistance.
