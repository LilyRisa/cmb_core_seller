# Product Overview

The Seller Leads Pool for partners, accessible within the Partner Center, provides a online centralized platform for partners to submit and manage their submitted seller leads. It is designed to streamline the lead validation, matching, and nomination process, enhancing efficiency in strategic key merchant (SKM) recruitment and onboarding.  
The system supports bulk lead submission, automated pre-validation, and provides actionable feedback for lead management.  
This system is available for partners which have joined Project Horizon in US. Check more about the project introduction. [\[External\] Project Horizon-Quarterly Incentive](https://bytedance.larkoffice.com/wiki/MaJZwCy4qi7GzXkL8TEcRGhGnkd)

# Product Benefits

The Seller Lead Pool submitted by partners provides two primary advantages:

-   **Enhanced Operational Efficiency and Compliance**: By migrating the lead management process from offline spreadsheets and manual communication to a centralized online system, it delivers significant improvements in compliance, efficiency, and convenience for all parties.
    
-   **Partner Empowerment in Seller development**: The system empowers partners by providing a streamlined tool for key seller recruitment and incubation. This enhances their operational leverage and productivity in identifying and onboarding high-potential sellers.

# FAQ

1.  **Is there a limit to how many leads can be uploaded at once?**

Yes, a single file for bulk upload cannot exceed 2,000 lead entries. For larger lead lists, the file must be split into multiple smaller files that each adhere to this limit.

1.  All offline submitted leads in Q1 have been automatically uploaded to your leads pool. Agencies can check the leads along with seller follow-up suggestions.
