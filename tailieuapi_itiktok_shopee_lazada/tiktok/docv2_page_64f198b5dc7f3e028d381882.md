# Introduction

The Shop Management page is where partners can view and manage all their authorizations with sellers.

# Guide

Once the seller has authorized Apps or Services, DTSP will be able to check and manage authorization on the *Seller Management - Manage* Authorize *page*  
Tips: Partners can use the filters below to locate specific authorizations. eg authorization ID, service name, shop name/code/id  
![Image](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/07f8649b1dbb422a926ecb020b0fdf75~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)  
If one seller has renewed the authorization with TSP, TSP will be able to check the seller's extended authorization on the authorization list page as below.  
![Image](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/40ea59530e604c5fbdc6f15adbeced59~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)  
By clicking "View More", Partner can check the authorization under “Related Authorization".  
![Image](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/ed90ca2de53840a59bb0e8af4f0e9182~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)
