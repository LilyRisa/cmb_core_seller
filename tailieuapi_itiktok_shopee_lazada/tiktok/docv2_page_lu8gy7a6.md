# **Introduction**

The Partner Growth Task Center (Partner - TTSPC) is an exclusive task management platform for partner users. Its entrance is located on the homepage of the "Growth Center" within the Partner Center, divided into two modules: "Task" and "Growth Task". This center is only accessible to partner roles (Seller Service- *TSP/* Creator Service\*- CAP\*/ Creator Matchmaking- *TAP*) logging in through main accounts or authorized sub-accounts.

# **What Are the Benefits?**

Centralized task hub in Partner Center enables easy access to policies and tasks, paired with real-time progress tracking and self-service.  
Gain a partner conversion rate and an expanded base of high-quality partners to strengthen its ecosystem, increase total agency GMV and form a scalable, modular framework that supports future expansion.
