duration\_type‚Äč

 | 

string‚Äč

 | 

The effective time type of the current activity‚Äč

\- NORMAL: begin\_time and end\_time must be filled‚Äč

\- INDEFINITE: begin\_time and end\_time aren't required, and the promotion will be a long-term activity which runs indefinitely. ‚Äč

1\. INDEFINITE is only valid when activity\_type is SHIPPING\_DISCOUNT,‚Äč

2\. If duration\_type is INDEFINITE, start\_time and end\_time should be 0‚Äč

Default: NORMAL‚Äč
