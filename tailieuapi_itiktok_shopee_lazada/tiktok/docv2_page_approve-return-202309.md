decision‚Äč

Required‚Äč

 | 

string‚Äč

 | 

Seller's decision for the return request, available values:‚Äč

\- APPROVE\_REFUND: seller approve a refund-only request‚Äč

\- APPROVE\_RETURN: seller approve the initial return-and-refund request‚Äč

\- APPROVE\_RECEIVED\_PACKAGE: seller approve return-and-refund request after the buyer has already shipped or handed over the item.‚Äč

\- APPROVE\_REPLACEMENT: seller approve replacement request‚Äč

\- ISSUE\_REPLACEMENT\_REFUND: seller decline the replacement request and directly refund the buyer instead.‚Äč

\- OFFER\_PARTIAL\_REFUND: seller offers a partial refund amount to the buyer without requiring the item(s) to be returned.‚Äč

\- DIRECT\_REFUND: seller issues a returnless refund, meaning the buyer receives a full refund and keeps the item‚Äč

 |
