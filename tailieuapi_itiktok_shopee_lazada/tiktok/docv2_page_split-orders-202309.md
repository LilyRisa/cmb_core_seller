Split Orders

вЂ‹Shop

вЂ‹

Required scope:

вЂ‹seller.fulfillment.package.write

вЂ‹

Use this API to confirm an order split. Note that вЂ‹вЂ‹supported split levels vary by regionвЂ‹вЂ‹:вЂ‹

\- Some regions support вЂ‹вЂ‹item-level splitsвЂ‹вЂ‹ (splitting individual units of the same SKU).вЂ‹

\- Others only support вЂ‹вЂ‹all-units splitsвЂ‹вЂ‹ (splitting different SKUs into separate packages).вЂ‹

вЂ‹

Here are two examples of supported splits:вЂ‹

\- вЂ‹вЂ‹Case 1: all-units splitвЂ‹вЂ‹, applicable for orders in BR, SEA, MX (local sellers)вЂ‹

Split a buyer order of SKU A of quantity 2 and SKU B of quantity 1 into two separate packages:вЂ‹

\- вЂ‹вЂ‹Package 1вЂ‹вЂ‹: all units of SKU AвЂ‹

вЂ‹вЂ‹ - Package 2вЂ‹вЂ‹: all units of SKU BвЂ‹

вЂ‹

\- вЂ‹вЂ‹Case 2: item-level splitвЂ‹вЂ‹, applicable for orders in EU, JP, MX (global sellers), UK, USвЂ‹

Split the same order contents into three individual packages:вЂ‹

\- вЂ‹вЂ‹Package 1вЂ‹вЂ‹: 1 unit of SKU AвЂ‹

вЂ‹ - вЂ‹Package 2вЂ‹вЂ‹: 1 unit of SKU AвЂ‹

вЂ‹вЂ‹ - Package 3вЂ‹вЂ‹: 1 unit of SKU BвЂ‹
