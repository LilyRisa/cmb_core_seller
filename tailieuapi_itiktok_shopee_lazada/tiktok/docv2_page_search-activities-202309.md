Activity status.вЂ‹

Possible values:вЂ‹

\- DRAFT: Promotion activities with this status are not available to TikTok users.вЂ‹

\- NOT\_START: Promotion activities with this status are not available to TikTok users until the set activity start time.вЂ‹

\- ONGOING: Promotion activities with this status are available to TikTok users.вЂ‹

\- EXPIRED: Promotion activities with this status are not available to TikTok users because it has expired.вЂ‹

\- DEACTIVATED: The activity has been deactivated by the seller and is not available to TikTok users.вЂ‹

\- NOT\_EFFECTIVE: The activity is terminated by the platform and is not available to TikTok users.вЂ‹
