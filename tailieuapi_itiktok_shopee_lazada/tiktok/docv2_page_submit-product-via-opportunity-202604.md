Submit Product via Opportunity

вЂ‹Shop

вЂ‹

Required scope:

вЂ‹seller.product.basic

вЂ‹

Submit an existing product (already listed on TikTok Shop) as a response to a specific opportunity lead. The product will be linked to the opportunity and enter the standard submission review flow.вЂ‹

If you need to list a new product to meet the opportunity lead, you must first request the product release API to create the product\_id , and then submit the product to link the opportunity lead.вЂ‹
