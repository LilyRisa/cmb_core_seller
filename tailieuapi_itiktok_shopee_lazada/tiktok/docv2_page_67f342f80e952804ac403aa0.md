# Introduction

This tool allows TSPs to request the cancellation of service authorization. If the seller agrees to the cancellation or fails to respond within 72 hours, the authorization will be automatically terminated.

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/a28510132c884f6ba112571242384862~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

# Step 1: TSP raises a cancellation request

TSP can navigate to ++TikTok Shop Partner Center-> Seller Management++ and click terminate++ ++to raise a cancel authorization request.  
![Image](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/6ef558997b7f4b6d95f1e7e55dce01dc~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)  
![Image](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/a3f7e76f37ce430c99275905d4e76050~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)  
TSP should fill in the related reason for cancellation and leave a note if needed. Which would make it easier for the seller to check and review.

# Step 2: Seller checks and replies to requests.

After a TSP initiates a cancellation, the seller can view the request on the My Service page:

1.  If the seller chooses "Agree," the authorization will be canceled immediately.
2.  If the seller chooses "Reject," the authorization will remain valid. You may contact the seller or resubmit your request after 144 hours.
3.  **If the seller does not respond within 72 hours, the authorization will be automatically canceled.**

# Step 3: Check the status of the cancellation request

TSP can check the cancellation status on the seller management page, and click view details to check the action records  
![Image](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/6216d460405a4be18fa556597ed6bbbc~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)  
![Image](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/6cb80e9bc5a34724a12291aac5fdc5df~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)  
The seller can check the cancellation record on My Service page.
