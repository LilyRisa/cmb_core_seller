# **Steps to use Seller-Creator IM**

TSP can utilize the features of Seller-Creator IM if authorized for Full Access or Affiliation.  
After logging into the Seller Center via TSP Access, on the “Affiliate” page, click the “Go to Affiliate Center Home” button to enter the Affiliate Center.

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/a95d4a8789f741c89d9d8014958c5c7e~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

Once in the Affiliate Center, TSPs can use Creator IM just like sellers do through 5 entrances. Please refer to the [Seller - Creator IM User Guide](https://seller-us.tiktok.com/university/essay?course_type=1&from=search{contentIdParams}&identity=1&is_new_connect=0&is_new_user=0&knowledge_id=2725932827150123&role=1).

-   **Entrance 1:** In the Affiliate Center, click on Creator messages at the top navigation bar.

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/e0aed9decdc44a9fa62d46ff162d46a9~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

-   **Entrance 2:** Send IMs to creators directly within Find creators in Affiliate Center.

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/b97dc7c75ef94680a9cb3834b50e59c1~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

-   **Entrance 3:** Send IMs to creators directly within their detailed profile view.

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/3a8862cad1204cbaa4a1ea7e703754fb~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

-   **Entrance 4:** Share a specific product via an IM within the targeted collaboration page.

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/1c46fa635b854da695fb18570f2a02eb~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

-   **Entrance 5:** Send IMs to creators by clicking on the speech bubble icon on the Sample Request page.

![](https://p16-arcosite-sg.ibyteimg.com/tos-alisg-i-k9wyc2ijk0-sg/2d14474c939b47869d519438d3ffe42d~tplv-k9wyc2ijk0-image.image)

## FAQ

**Q1: Can I utilize the sub-account in the seller center and TSP access simultaneously?**  
In the same Chrome profile, you can either use tsp access or the Seller Center sub-account, which implies that you have to log out if you wish to switch to the other.
